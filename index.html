Safety Guidelines - Hive App

  1. Privacy Settings: Customize your privacy settings to control who can view your profile and interact with you on Hive. Be mindful of the information you share and adjust settings according to your comfort level.
  2. Content Moderation: Hive promotes a positive and inclusive environment. Please refrain from sharing inappropriate or offensive content. Our content moderation team works diligently to ensure a safe space for all users.
  3. Personal Information: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information, such as your address or financial details, with other users. Hive will never ask for such information within the app.
  4. Reporting and Blocking: If you encounter any behavior that violates our community guidelines or makes you uncomfortable, use the reporting and blocking features. Our support team will investigate reports promptly.
  5. Age Verification: Hive is intended for users of a certain age group. Ensure that you meet the age requirements to use the app. If you have concerns or questions, reach out to our support team.
  6. Offline Meetups: Exercise caution when considering offline meetups with other Hive users. If you choose to meet in person, do so in a public place and inform someone you trust about your plans.
  7. Device Security: Keep your device's security features enabled, such as passcodes and biometric authentication, to protect your Hive account.
  8. Unauthorized Access: Do not share your Hive account credentials with anyone. Hive representatives will never ask for your password. If you suspect unauthorized access, change your password immediately.
